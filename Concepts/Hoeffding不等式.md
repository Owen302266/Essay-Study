对于独立同分布随机变量$\mathrm{u}_{1}, \mathrm{u}_{2}, \cdots, \mathrm{u}_{M} \sim p(u)$，如果$\mathrm{E}\left[\mathrm{u}_{i}\right]=\mu \text { and } \operatorname{Pr}\left[a \leq \mathrm{u}_{i} \leq b\right]=1 \text {, }$，则可以得到大偏差不等式：$$\operatorname{Pr}\left[\left|\frac{1}{M} \sum_{m=1}^{M} \mathrm{u}_{m}-\mu\right|>\epsilon\right] \leq 2 \exp \left(-\frac{2 M \epsilon^{2}}{(b-a)^{2}}\right)$$
